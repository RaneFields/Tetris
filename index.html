TETRIS




Instructions:


Blocks will spawn at the top of the screen. It is your job to manuever them into rows as they fall to the bottom. For every row completed, you will gain points, and the row will disappear. Multiple rows cleared at the same time are worth more points.


Rotates Blocks


Moves Blocks Left


Moves Blocks Right


Moves Blocks Down Faster


Moves Blocks Down Instantly




Points:

  • 1 line: 100
  • 2 lines: 250
  • 3 lines: 500
  • 4 lines (Tetris): 800
  • back to back Tetris: 1200 each
